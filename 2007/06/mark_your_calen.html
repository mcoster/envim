Mark your calendar: next Plastics Roundup is Sept. 29 & Oct. 6

This time around the Master Recycler Plastics Roundup will be split over two weekends:

Eastside: Saturday, September 29 from 9am-2pm

  • Floyd Light Middle School, 10800 SE Washington
  • Kaiser Permanente, 3550 N Interstate (at Fremont)

Westside: Saturday, October 6 from 9am-2pm

  • PCC Sylvania, 12000 SW 49th Lot 10
  • Westview High School, 4200 NW 185th North Parking Lot
