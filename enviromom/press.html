press

EnviroMom.com has appeared in various media since our launch in March 2007, and founders Heather Hawkins and Renee Limon are available for interviews. Please contact them at enviromompdx [at] gmail [dot] com.

Nightline (ABC), November 21, 2008 -- Take Out the Trash by Lisa Fletcher

Renee Limon and Heather Hawkins discuss household waste reduction and share tips and practices for reducing, reusing and recycling around the house. The EnviroMoms give another Portland family a 'garbage makeover' and shop a local grocery store to point out ways to reduce food packaging waste.

Marketplace (American Public Media), October 13, 2008 -- Green voters on the coming election by Mitchell Hartman

A group of EnviroMoms discuss the issues that are important to them and the role that government should play in the sustainability movement.

Sustainable Life magazine, November 2008 -- Brilliant Innovations: EnviroMom.com Mothers Teach Themselves, and Their Kids How to Live Lightly by Audrey Van Buskirk

Heather Hawkins and Renee Limon discuss how the baby step approach of EnviroMom.com can influence the greening of moms (and their buying power) across the nation.

Portland Monthly Magazine, October 2008 -- Ecofreaks by Brian Barker

Heather Hawkins and Renee Limon are two of six 'Ecofreaks' photographed and featured in Portland Monthly's green issue. They discuss their One Can A Month Challenge and distaste for corn-based plastics.

Metro Parent, Your Baby & You, 2008-2009 -- Growing a 'Green' Baby by Anne Laufe

With so many green products targeted at new parents, how do you make the right choices for your family? Heather and Renee weigh in on baby showers, hand-me-downs and supportive green networks.

The Oregonian, August 21, 2008 -- Dozens join challenge to keep a lid on trash by Rebecca Koffman

The EnviroMoms discuss their One Can A Month Challenge, and a family that successfully reduced their garbage service to once monthly is profiled.

The Oregonian, July 24, 2008 -- Blogs and books help parents to teach green by Jenn Director Knudsen

Green parenting resources and tips for raising green kids are the focus of this article. Heather and Renee talk about their own children's reduce / reuse / recycle habits.

KOIN News 6, June 25, 2008 -- Hanging Out

Renee Limon discusses the money and energy-savings that come with using a clothesline rather than a dryer.

Portland Tribune, June 12, 2007 -- Moms hop online for local-global links by Jo Ostgarden

With the blog gaining greater notice, Heather and Renee talk about the importance of baby steps when making green lifestyle changes and how GreenGroups can make the process more enjoyable.
