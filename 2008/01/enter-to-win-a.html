Enter to win a PH12.6 gift pack!

Woo hoo! One of our sponsors, PH12.6, has graciously offered to give FIVE lucky EnviroMoms a gift pack containing one 16.9 fl oz bottle of PH12.6, a smaller 2 fl oz bottle and a terry cleaning cloth. We've written about this stuff before -- I love it because it cuts through kitchen grease and makes my black granite countertops sparkly clean and smudge-free. Renee has used it countless times on paint, stinky smells and other applications. The product is just ionized water, which means, er, it's been ionized? It's like it's been highly charged to where it has a high alkaline content, so it can break through dirt and grease, which has a high acid content (I think). It doesn't work on water stains, which are alkaline, but it does serious work on just about any other kind of mess. Love it!

Sooooo, as with all of our drawings, we print out the entries and pick the winners at random. Send us an email at enviromompdx [at] gmail [dot] com by noon this Thursday, Jan. 10 and tell us one of your green resolutions for 2008. What lifestyle changes or green products or kid-centric activities do you hope to incorporate into your lives?
