Tapped, the movie

I'm excited about the documentary Tapped, and the trailer has got me all worked up. (The images of the plastic in the ocean make me sick.) I've had a tentative toe on the anti-plastic bus, but this film might just get me fully on board, buckled up and ready to roar. It's produced by the folks who made Who Killed the Electric Car, an excellent, albeit maddening, documentary. I can find no info on when this movie will be released, but it apparently debuted at the Sundance Festival in January. Stay tuned...
